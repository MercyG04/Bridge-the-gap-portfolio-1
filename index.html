Mapping Digital Skills in Kenya...

Join us on an innovative journey as we map the landscape of digital skills training across Kenya. Our project, "Bridging the Digital Skills Gap in Kenya," is designed to connect learners with opportunities and transform lives through digital education.

This interactive platform shines a light on the diverse training initiatives available, making it easier for Kenyans to access and benefit from digital skills programs.

In today's digital era, digital skills are vital. Our project bridges the gap by making training accessible across Kenya, empowering individuals to seize opportunities and boost economic growth.

Discover, Learn, and Grow

Explore digital skills training locations across Kenya, access detailed program information, be inspired by success stories, and access resources for continuous learning support.
